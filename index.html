Yuta Iwatani

I am a third-year Ph.D. student Aerodynamic Design Research Group at Tohoku University. My current research numerically investigates how wall temperature affects boundary layer transition in the transonic regime, which is crucial for improving the efficiency and safety of high-speed aircraft. Beyond this, I am also developing advanced analysis methods, specifically based on resolvent analysis, to better understand and control intricate flow dynamics. Currently, I am awarded by the JSPS (Japan Society for the Promotion of Science) Research Fellowship for Young Scientists (DC1).
