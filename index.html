Yuta Iwatani

I am a second-year PhD student affiliated with the Aerodynamic Design Research Group at Tohoku University in Japan. I am awarded by the JSPS (Japan Society for the Promotion of Science) Research Fellowship for Young Scientists (DC1). My current research interest is persuing the physics in the compressible flow dynamics with the aid of high-fidelity numerical simulation and modal analysis to provide the insights/tools for the design of aircraft and/or the effective way of flow control in engineering.Contact yuta.iwatani(at)dc.tohoku.ac.jp
