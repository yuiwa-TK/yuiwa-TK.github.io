Our new paper  ["Detecting Turbulent Breakdown for Predicting Transitional Boundary Layers in Wall-Modeled Large-Eddy Simulation"](https://doi.org/10.2514/1.J066244) which I contributed to as a co-first author has appeared in AIAA Journal! 
We proposed a sensor that detects turbulent breakdown associated with the rapid increase in skin friction to activate the wall model. 
The proposed sensor-based wall-model calculation achieves high fidelity simulations of various scenarios of boundary layer transitions subject to wall heating/cooling with significantly reduced cost compared to the DNS!
